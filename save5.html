1

2. The second triangle has the pattern
1
1 1

The missing scientist Ada Lovelace is stuck at some year in the future. Sources say that she’s currently in the UTC timestamp T, where T is the last ten digits of the first element of the Nth row in the first triangle. N is the ceil(X/2) element in the Xth row of the second triangle. X is the answer obtained in the previous question. You need to travel to the future to rescue Ada Lovelace. The time stamp required can be obtained performing an UTC timestamp conversion on T. What is the timestamp?
