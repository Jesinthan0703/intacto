How to submit answers!
1) Use '^' to mention exponents. Example: 2^8 is 28
2) Use '*' to specify multiplication.
3)Leave a blank space in between terms Example 456 * 2^7.
4) Enter the units in the second textbox.
5) The scientist's name should be in lowercase. Example : newton.
6) The symbol must in uppercase. Example: N.
7) Answers can also be expressed without exponents. Example: 9 * 10^2 can be 900.
